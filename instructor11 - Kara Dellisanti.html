Kara Dellisanti Instructor Biography

Kara Dellisanti is currently obtaining her Master’s in Curriculum and Instruction from American College of Education. Before coming to West-MEC as a C&I Specialist, Kara was an English Language Arts instructor in the Glendale Union High School District for seven years. She is passionate about encouraging young and developing teachers in their classroom practices, particularly in embedding literacy strategies and student engagement strategies into the CTE curriculum. She also has a passion for writing music and prose, spending quality time with her loved ones, and training her two rescue dogs.
