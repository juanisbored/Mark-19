OSHA Training & Certification

West-MEC is proud to provide OSHA Training and Certification from our OSHA Outreach Program Trainers. Below are three groups for which we provide training.


West-MEC Teachers

  • West-MEC provides OSHA General or OSHA Healthcare for all programs.
  • Instructors must work with their Administrator to secure funding prior to securing dates.
  • Requires 10 hours of instructional time.

School Districts

  • We provide OSHA General and Healthcare.
  • $28 per student.
  • $58 per adult.
  • Requires 10 hours of instructional time over 2 days.

Business/Industry/Community

  • Secure West-MEC for OSHA certification at your location or one of ours.
  • $58 per adult with a minimum of 15-to maximum of 40-persons.
  • Individualized training based on availability.
